When is the Right Time to Get a Life Insurance?

Getting a life insurance is not just getting another policy, instead it is getting a mental piece a reassurance that your loved ones and your dependants’ financial interests are preserved even after if you are not there to look after for them.

This has a simple answer: The right time is before you need it. Yes the life insurance bought at its earliest is the most suitable deal, the younger the healthier you are, the easier it is to lock in affordable premiums. Beyond that, the “right time” often comes down to life events where others depend on you financially like:

  • Buying a Home or Taking a Mortgage
  • Starting a family / Having Children
  • Getting Married
  • New job
  • Starting a business

Other Important Insurance Options Available

Critical Illness Cover

Provides a tax-free lump sum if you’re diagnosed with a serious illness (like cancer, stroke, heart attack, MS, etc.). Helps with treatment costs, living expenses, or mortgage repayments while you recover.

Income Protection Cover

Replaces part of your income if you’re unable to work due to illness or injury. Usually pays monthly until you’re well enough to return to work or reach retirement age.

Family Protection Insurance

Broad category that ensures financial stability for dependents — can be structured as a lump sum, monthly benefit, or combination.
